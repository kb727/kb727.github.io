WHAT'S KB727

KB727 is a global certificate program that advocates for social responsibility in tutoring.

Our non-profit is dedicated to empowering students by providing them with opportunities to transform their futures.

Many of these students can't afford the essential fees for tutoring that could unlock life-changing doors. That's why we're working to help them take the crucial next step toward success.

WHY KB727

Money shouldn't dictate access to education. We want to live in a world where everyone has access to a great education — not just those whose families can afford one. We encourage and support more low-income families and those with financial difficulties to access free tutoring. As of December 2024, we have booked more than 200 interviews in Shanghai, Seattle and Buffalo, and have awarded 17 certificates.

HOW WE WORK

The same tutor who provides free tutoring for low-income families and the financially disadvantaged for more than 6 months will receive the KB727 certificate and rewards, as well as commercial promotion support. Authentication documents and delivery of reward items will be confirmed by official email. We're the oldest organization doing this work. We've been doing this for a long time, and we've learned a lot along the way.

WANT TO GET INVOLVED?

We need your help to share KB727 with your network and create a lasting impact. Education has the power to transform lives, uplift communities, and break the cycle of poverty. With your support, we can give these students the chance they deserve.

It will only take a few minutes to copy the message we provide and share it with your network.

We are willing to cooperate with more tutors, media, institutions, and donors to jointly provide support for socially responsible tutoring. If you're ready to help, please reach out. Together, we can make a world of difference. Thank you.
