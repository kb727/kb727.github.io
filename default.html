KB727 - Forging Advanced Manufacturing Hubs

KB727 is an international industrial real estate firm headquartered in Shanghai, China. We specialize in fostering regional economic growth by driving industrial investment promotion​ to help build robust key industrial chains​ and vibrant industrial clusters. Our approach includes organizing exclusive one-on-one industry salons​ annually in major economic hubs like Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Shenzhen, and Dongguan, facilitating direct dialogue and collaboration between enterprises and local ecosystems.

We warmly welcome partnerships and exchanges in advanced manufacturing, with a focus on the following sectors:

1. High-End New Materials

R&D and application of advanced rare-earth functional materials, high-quality special steels, high-performance alloys, high-purity rare metal materials, carbon fiber composites, bio-based and biomedical materials, metallocene polyethylene, and high-purity electronic materials like photoresists for integrated circuits.

2. Major Technical Equipment

Development and application of CR450 high-speed standard electric multiple units, standardized metro trains, high-end machine tools, advanced engineering machinery, key components for nuclear power units, cruise ships, large LNG carriers, deep-sea oil and gas platforms, demonstration operations of the C919 large passenger aircraft, and serial development of the ARJ21 regional jet.

3. Intelligent Manufacturing and Robotics

Development of distributed control systems, programmable logic controllers, data acquisition, and video monitoring systems. Key technology applications for intelligent robots, including advanced controllers, high-precision servo drives, high-performance reducers, and additive manufacturing.

4. Aero-Engines and Gas Turbines

R&D and verification of key technologies for advanced aero-engine materials, product development of the CJ1000 large-bypass-ratio turbofan engine, application of key technologies for wide-body aircraft engines, industrialization of advanced civil turboshaft engines, and the construction of test power stations for heavy-duty gas turbines.

5. BeiDou Industrial Applications

Application of integrated communication and navigation technologies, innovation platforms for BeiDou applications, its use in sectors like communications, finance, energy, and civil aviation, and its market-scale application in consumer areas such as car navigation, smartphones, and wearable devices.

6. New Energy Vehicles (NEVs) and Intelligent Connected Vehicles (ICVs)

Application of key technologies for high-safety power batteries, efficient drive motors, and high-performance powertrains for NEVs. R&D on basic technology platforms, software/hardware systems, wire-controlled chassis, and smart terminals for ICVs.

7. High-End Medical Equipment and Innovative Drugs

R&D of core technologies for endoscopic surgical robots and ECMO machines. Development of high-end medical imaging, radiotherapy equipment, key components, implants like brain pacemakers and fully degradable vascular stents, and upgraded rehabilitation aids. R&D for vaccines against major infectious diseases and specific drugs for cancers and cardiovascular diseases. Development of key technologies and equipment for traditional Chinese medicine.

8. Agricultural Machinery Equipment

Development of intelligent high-horsepower tractors, precision seeders, sprayers, fertilizer applicators, efficient combine harvesters, fruit and vegetable harvesters, sugarcane harvesters, cotton pickers, and specialized agricultural machinery for hilly areas. R&D and industrialization of advanced grain and oil processing equipment, and intelligent green breeding equipment for feeding, environment control, monitoring, and manure utilization. Equipment for afforestation and grassland improvement.

KB727 is committed to building bridges for innovation and investment in these cutting-edge fields. Let's collaborate to drive industrial transformation and growth.
