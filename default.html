KB727 - Forging Advanced Manufacturing Hubs

KB727 is an international industrial real estate firm headquartered in Shanghai, China. We specialize in strategic investment promotion and ecosystem development. We partner with regional governments to build and strengthen key industrial chains​ and vibrant advanced manufacturing clusters​ across China. Our platform facilitates this growth through dedicated matchmaking sessions and one-on-one networking events, fostering collaboration and technological exchange.

We warmly welcome partnerships, particularly in the following sectors of advanced manufacturing:

1. High-End New Materials

Focusing on the R&D and application of advanced materials, including high-end rare-earth functional materials, high-quality special steels, performance alloys, high-purity rare metals, advanced ceramics, electronic glass, carbon fiber and aramid composites, bio-based and biomedical materials, metallocene polyethylene, and high-purity electronic materials like photoresists for integrated circuits.

2. Major Technical Equipment

Supporting the development and application of key equipment such as the CR450 high-speed standard train series, standardized metro trains, high-end machine tools, advanced engineering machinery, key components for nuclear power units, cruise ships, large LNG carriers, and deep-sea oil and gas platforms. We also engage in the demonstration operations of the C919 passenger aircraft and the serial development of the ARJ21 regional jet.

3. Intelligent Manufacturing and Robotics

Engaging in the development of industrial control equipment like distributed control systems and programmable logic controllers, alongside key robotics technologies such as advanced controllers, high-precision servo drives, high-performance reducers, and additive manufacturing (3D printing) .

4. Aero-Engines and Gas Turbines

Involved in the R&D and validation of key technologies for advanced aero-engines, the development of commercial high-bypass turbofan engines like the CJ1000, and the industrialization of heavy-duty gas turbines.

5. BeiDou Industrial Applications

Promoting the integrated application of BeiDou navigation technology across various industries, including communications, finance, energy, and civil aviation, as well as its large-scale use in consumer domains like vehicle navigation, smartphones, and wearable devices.

6. New Energy Vehicles (NEVs) and Intelligent Connected Vehicles (ICVs)

Focusing on key technologies for new energy vehicles, including high-safety batteries, efficient drive motors, and high-performance powertrains, alongside the R&D of foundational platforms and key components like smart hardware systems and wire-controlled chassis for ICVs.

7. High-End Medical Equipment and Innovative Drugs

Supporting the R&D of core technologies for advanced medical devices and innovative pharmaceuticals, including robotic surgical systems, ECMO machines, high-end medical imaging and radiotherapy equipment, implants, vaccines, and novel drugs for critical diseases.

8. Agricultural Machinery Equipment

Driving the development of advanced agricultural machinery, such as high-horsepower intelligent tractors, precision planters, efficient combine harvesters, and specialized equipment for hilly areas, as well as smart breeding and farming technologies.

KB727 serves as a dynamic bridge connecting global innovation with local industrial capacity. We are committed to co-creating the future of manufacturing through strategic partnerships.
